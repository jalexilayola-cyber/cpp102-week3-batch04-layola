PROBLEM 5 ALGORITHM

Step 1: Start.
Step 2: Ask the user to enter the price and quantity of the first item. Store it as price1 and quantity1.
Step 3: Ask the user to enter the price and quantity of the second item. Store it as price2 and quantity2.
Step 4: Ask the user to enter the price and quantity of the third item. Store it as price3 and quantity3.
Step 5: Compute total cost = (price1 * quantity1) +
                             (price2 * quantity2) +
                             (price3 * quantity3).
Step 6: Display total cost.
Step 7: End.

PROBLEM 6 ALGORITHM

Step 1: Start.
Step 2: Ask the user to enter the daily budget. Store it as budget.
Step 3: Ask the user to enter the number of days. Store it as days.
Step 4: Ask the user to enter the daily transportation allowance. Store it as transportation.
Step 5: Compute monthly budget = (budget * days) + (transportation * days).
Step 6: Display monthly budget.
Step 7: End.
